A history of surfing

Introduction

The sport of surfing involves riding a wave on some buoyant surface. There are many skill levels to surfing as well as many types of surfboards. Long boards, short boards, quad fins, single fins, the list can go on and on... Hawaii is a surfing powerhouse as we are surrounded by steep reef shelves and warm water.
